Hostel Rules and Regulations

All residents are expected to abide by the following rules and regulations to ensure a safe, peaceful, and clean living environment:

  1. Quiet Hours

    Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. During this time, residents must maintain a quiet environment to avoid disturbing others.

  2. Visitors

    Visitors are allowed during designated visiting hours. All visitors must register at the front desk and obtain a visitor's pass.

  3. Cleanliness

    Residents are responsible for maintaining cleanliness in their rooms and common areas. Littering and untidy behavior are strictly prohibited.

  4. Smoking and Alcohol

    Smoking and consumption of alcohol are strictly prohibited within the hostel premises.

  5. Damage to Property

    Residents are liable for any damage caused to hostel property and will be charged accordingly.
